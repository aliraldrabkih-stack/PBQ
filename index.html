Security+ PBQ Lessons

The purpose of Performance-Based Questions (PBQs) is to evaluate a candidate’s practical, hands-on skills by placing them in real-world cybersecurity scenarios that simulate workplace environments. PBQs measure not just theoretical knowledge, but the ability to apply security concepts, analyze situations, and implement solutions effectively under realistic conditions.
